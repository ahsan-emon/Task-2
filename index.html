A national initiative is being made by the Ministry of the Interior in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to issue and manage the digital IDs of citizens and residents. Since many governmental and private agencies have started to keep pace with the development and to provide its services through electronic portals by using electronic identifiers (username and password) through using a verifying means that allow access for its electronic services, which are actually considered as digital IDs, that is linked to the individual and represents his identity in electronic transactions, which is called a "digital identity".
